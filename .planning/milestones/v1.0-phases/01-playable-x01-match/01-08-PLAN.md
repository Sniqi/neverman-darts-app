<objective>
Close CR-06: `MatchStore.remaining` returns `this.activePlayer?.remaining`, which is the
start-of-visit value (the reducer intentionally commits `remaining` only at visit end). During a
board visit after dart 1 or 2, both the displayed score and the checkout suggestion still reflect
the pre-visit remaining — e.g. a player on 100 who hits T20 still sees 100 and the suggestion
"T20 D20" instead of 40 and "D20". This violates the D-10 live-recalculation requirement (ENG-07).

The fix makes the `remaining` getter subtract the running total of `state.currentVisit` from the
committed `activePlayer.remaining`. Because `suggestion` is derived from `this.remaining`, fixing
the getter fixes the live checkout suggestion at the same time. Numpad visits commit immediately
(currentVisit stays empty for numpad), so they are unaffected.

Purpose: Make the remaining score and checkout suggestion recalculate live after every dart in a
board visit (ENG-07, D-10).
Output: A corrected `remaining` getter in `match.svelte.ts` and a unit test that locks live
mid-visit recalculation.
</objective>
