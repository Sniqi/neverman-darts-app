<objective>
Close three remaining defects that block the scoring and setup truths, each a small surgical change:

- CR-02 (ENG-07): `ScorePanel.svelte` renders `{player.remaining}` (the committed start-of-visit value) for the active player. The live `matchStore.remaining` getter (which subtracts the current board visit) exists but no UI consumes it. Result: after dart 1 or 2 on the board, the big score still shows the old value while the CheckoutSuggestion beside it (which uses the live getter) shows the updated route — the two contradict each other.
- CR-05 (FLOW-01): `BullOffOrder.confirmOrder()` dispatches `START_MATCH` unconditionally even when `order` is empty (direct `/bulloff` navigation, or browser-back after a match removes `sessionStorage.pendingMatch`). The reducer then creates a 0-player match and the first `DART_THROWN` reads `state.players[0].remaining` on `undefined`, crashing the scoring view.
- WR-01 (INP-03): the winning `NUMPAD_VISIT` is dispatched before the darts-at-double dialog opens, with `dartsAtDouble: 0`, so the dialog's confirmation is discarded — D-08 darts-at-double capture is non-functional end-to-end.

Purpose: Live mid-visit score (ENG-07), crash-proof setup flow (FLOW-01), and functional darts-at-double capture (INP-03).
Output: Three corrected wirings plus reducer hardening and tests.
</objective>
