## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-03 | Tampering (XSS) | StatCard / StatDrawer rendering player names or labels | mitigate | All strings rendered via Svelte `{interpolation}` only — never `{@html}` (T-03-05 convention). Stat values are numbers from trusted engine state — no injection surface. |
| T-04-04 | Denial of Service | drawer recompute on every reactive tick | accept | `$derived.by` recomputes pure functions over a small visits array; cost is trivial at home-play scale. No mitigation needed. |

No network, no auth, no persistence in this plan. Other STRIDE categories are not applicable to a read-only display component.
