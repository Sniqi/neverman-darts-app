<objective>
Deliver the lifetime statistics slice (STAT-07, STAT-08): a new "Statistik" start-screen entry (D-10) leading to a per-profile dashboard with KPI tiles and hand-rolled SVG charts. Lifetime stats and records are recomputed from `db.matches` blobs (D-09) — there is NO separate records table (ACHV-03 is satisfied by recompute-from-history). Guests are excluded (D-11).

After this plan a player can do something new end-to-end: tap Statistik → choose a profile → view their lifetime averages, finish %, score bands, records, and trend charts.

Charts are hand-rolled SVG — no chart library (CLAUDE.md smallest-runtime; UI-SPEC explicit prohibition). This plan also produces `computeLifetimeStats`, which 04-04 consumes to preload each profile's records at match start.

Purpose: Make lifetime stats and the records dashboard reachable per D-10, recomputed from history per D-09.
Output: `db/stats.ts` (+ test), three SVG chart components, `ProfileStatDashboard`, the `/stats` route, and the start-screen menu entry.
</objective>

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
